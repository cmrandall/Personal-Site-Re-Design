About Me

After many years of hands on IT experience, I was introduced to Front-End Web Development. I started to learn through self-direction using various websites including:

  • www.teamtreehouse.com
  • www.lynda.com
  • www.codeschool.com

I quickly gained an interest and passion for coding websites and decided that I wanted to turn it into a career. I continue using the online resources to grow as a developer and create user friendly and accessible websites.

I am also a member of various Meetup groups through Meetup.com where I network and learn about current and upcoming standards across the field. Some of the groups I am a member of include:

  • Boston Front End Developers
  • Boston PHP
  • Cascade Boston
  • HTML5 Boston
  • Learn2Code

Through continued use of the websites and going to the meetups, I have learned how to use the latest website development technologies to craft websites to meet the W3C standards, as well as client standards and needs. Please feel free to take a look around my website to learn a little more about me and see how I have demonstrated my skill set. Don't hesitate to send me a message!
